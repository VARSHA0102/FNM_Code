About the Task

Our assessments are designed to be engaging and enjoyable. Each task encourages learning through fun challenges that stimulate creativity and critical thinking. We strive to make learning accessible and exciting for everyone!

Steps to Upload the Assessment:

1. If you're not registered, register. If already registered, log in.
2. You will be automatically redirected to the assessment page.
3. Understand the question.
4. After completing the task, upload the assignment in a ZIP file.
5. Click "Submit".
